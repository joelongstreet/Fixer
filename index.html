What is Fixer?

A tool for homebrewers which assists in correcting the specific gravity of wort immediately before entering the boil. To make an accurate recommendation, Fixer will need to know:

  • Current Gravity
  • Target gravity
  • Wort Temperature
  • Final Target Volume

How are calculations Made?

Fixer's calculations are based on this post. There are a few adjustments for unit conversion, but for the most part, it follows the same basic model.

What does 1.014 mean?

1.014 is the BJCP's maximum recommended final gravity for a California Common, my favorite style. You can read more about the style here.

Other 1.014 Apps

Fixer is currently the only 1.014 app. I'm working on more though :)
